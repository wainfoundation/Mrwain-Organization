Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Mrwain Organization?

The Mrwain Organization is a foundation advancing the Pi Network by developing beta DApps, providing transparent custodial wallets, and fostering community engagement.

How can I contribute to beta DApps?

You can contribute by joining our developer bounties, submitting code on GitHub, or providing feedback during beta testing. Visit our Community page for details.

Are the custodial wallets secure?

Yes, our wallets are audited by trusted partners like SecurePi, and transactions are trackable on the Pi Block Explorer. See our Ledger for details.

How do I donate to Mrwain?

Donate Pi through our secure donation portal to support beta DApp development and earn rewards like Hall of Fame recognition. Visit Donations to learn more.
