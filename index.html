About the Expanding Foundation

Founded in 2023, the Mrwain Organization is a growing foundation dedicated to advancing the Pi Network’s vision of a decentralized, inclusive economy. Our global team of developers, blockchain experts, and community advocates works tirelessly to build DApps that empower users and foster trust.

With a focus on transparency, we publish all wallet addresses, source code, and project updates for community scrutiny. Our foundation is expanding through partnerships, developer programs, and community initiatives, aiming to make Pi a cornerstone of the Web3 ecosystem.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I donate to Mrwain?

Donate Pi to our official wallet address via the Donation section. You’ll receive rewards like Hall of Fame recognition and early feature access.

How is transparency ensured?

All DApp wallet transactions are publicly trackable on the Pi Block Explorer, and our source code is open on GitHub.
