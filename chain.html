Our Blockchain Networks

Mrwain Organization is building innovative DApps on the Pi Network, with all projects in progress. We are also planning to expand to Stellar, Solana, and Ethereum soon.

Supported Blockchain Networks

Pi

Pi Network

In Progress

  • Quantum Pay (In Progress)

    A decentralized payment platform enabling fast, secure transactions using Pi across global merchants.

  • TruthWeb (In Progress)

    A blockchain-based social platform ensuring authentic, transparent content sharing within the Pi community.

  • Cloudy (In Progress)

    Secure, scalable cloud storage powered by Pi, offering encrypted data hosting for users and DApps.

  • Droplink (In Progress)

    A peer-to-peer file-sharing DApp leveraging Pi for fast, private, and decentralized transfers.

  • TaskHub (In Progress)

    A collaborative platform for managing tasks and projects, rewarding contributors with Pi tokens.

  • Salenus (In Progress)

    A decentralized Web3 social network built on the Pi Network, empowering users to connect, share, and build communities.

  • Trend Forge Update (In Progress)

    A decentralized news aggregator delivering verified updates on Pi ecosystem developments.

  • SmartSolve (In Progress)

    A decentralized platform utilizing AI to provide smart, real-time solutions for everyday challenges using Pi.

  • SundaySoul (In Progress)

    A decentralized wellness platform for mindful meditation and mental health practices, powered by Pi.
