Careers at Mrwain Organization

Join the Mrwain Organization to build innovative DApps for the Pi Network. Be part of a decentralized, mission-driven team shaping the future of Web3.

Why Work With Us?

At Mrwain, we’re passionate about creating open-source solutions that empower the global Pi Network community. Our team thrives on:

  • Innovation: Work on cutting-edge projects like Cloudy and TruthWeb.
  • Collaboration: Join a diverse, global team guided by our Code of Conduct.
  • Impact: Contribute to a decentralized ecosystem that prioritizes transparency and accessibility.
  • Flexibility: Enjoy remote work and flexible schedules to balance life and work.

Learn more about our mission on our About page.

Benefits & Perks

We value our team and offer benefits to support your growth and well-being:

  • Flexible Work: Fully remote with flexible hours.
  • Learning Opportunities: Access to blockchain and Web3 training resources.
  • Community Impact: Recognition in our Hall of Fame for significant contributions.
  • Collaboration Tools: Free access to premium tools for development and communication.

How to Apply

Ready to join us? Follow these steps:

  • Review our open positions above or check our Discord for updates.
  • Submit your resume and a brief cover letter via our Contact page.
  • Include links to your GitHub, portfolio, or relevant work samples.
  • Our team will reach out to schedule an interview if your skills align with our needs.

We’re committed to an inclusive hiring process, as outlined in our Code of Conduct.

Be a part of our mission to revolutionize the Pi Network. Apply today!
