Join Our Community

The Mrwain Organization thrives on the passion and collaboration of our global community. Whether you're a developer, designer, or Pi Network enthusiast, there’s a place for you to contribute and shape the future of Web3.

Why Join Us?

Our community is built on the values of transparency, innovation, and respect, as outlined in our Code of Conduct. By joining, you’ll:

  • Collaborate on cutting-edge DApps like Cloudy, TruthWeb, and QuantumPay.
  • Connect with a diverse group of developers and creators.
  • Help drive the growth of the Pi Network ecosystem.

Ways to Get Involved

There are many ways to contribute to the Mrwain Organization:

  • Contribute Code: Fork our GitHub repository, create a branch, and submit a Pull Request. See our FAQ for details.
  • Join Discussions: Participate in issues, pull requests, or community meetings on our repository or Discord.
  • Report Issues: Share bugs or vulnerabilities via our Security Policy.
  • Spread the Word: Follow us on X and share our projects with the Pi Network community.

Community Channels

Stay connected with us through our official channels:

  • GitHub: Track project progress and contribute.
  • Discord: Join real-time discussions and community events.
  • X: Follow for updates and announcements.
  • LinkedIn: Connect with our professional network.

Community Events

We host regular events to engage our community and foster collaboration:

  • Developer Meetups: Virtual sessions to discuss DApp development and share ideas.
  • Hackathons: Competitions to build new features for our DApps.
  • AMAs (Ask Me Anything): Live Q&A sessions with our team, announced on Announcements.

Check our Announcements page for upcoming events.

Ready to make an impact? Join our community today and help us build the future of the Pi Network!
